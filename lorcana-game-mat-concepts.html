100 Acre Wood Lorcana Mats!

Pooh's First Steps to Magic

It was a breezy day when Pooh Bear wandered a bit farther than usual in search of honey. The current fires had driven so much life from the once buzzing woods.

As he meandered through a quiet glade, he noticed something peculiar. A golden swirl of light dancing between the bees.

Curious (and always just a little hungry), Pooh reached out. The journey into the classes of the wood had begun.

Piglet's First Stand

The forest was quiet, that afternoon. Piglet wandered letting the trees guide him. A glint caught his eye, something stuck in the ground. Something, that he could use to help his friends in defending their home.

Tigger's First Taste of Glory

It was just before dusk in the Hundred Acre Wood when Tigger bounced his way to the tallest hill, chasing the sunset like it was a game. But something strange waited at the top. Half-buried in the grass, glinting with starlight, was a sword. A sword to train with.

Kanga Brew's Her First Potions

Setting up camp, Kanga sent Ru out to scavenge for specific ingredients. She knew her gifts were needed, her brews could heal even the most devastating wounds. Returning, Ru provided the ingredients, and with it, a hope for tomorrow.

Eeyore's First Peace

The evening was cold. Given the option to betray all he had once loved, Eeyore had to take some time to himself. He wandered until the light of the fireflies was all that permeated around him. There he remembered. There he knew, this forest was what he loved, and he could not let his friends down.
